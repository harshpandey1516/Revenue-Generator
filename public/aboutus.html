ABOUT HRC RESTAURANT

HRC Restaurant is one of the best biryani restaurant in Markapur . But menu is not limited to just biryani items . they serve more than 200 other savory dishes too . plus, the place is pretty budget friendly . Perfect for people who are just not done after a single plate of biryani . Which is most people . Because as any true biryani lover will know, there is no such things as too much biryani . It becomes quite population in a short span if time . Mostly because of their break fast buffets and gigantic thalis . On top of that, their menu features a surprise items every day .

We believe in quality and service and thereby strive hard to serve you better.
